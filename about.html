Welcome to the Department of Computer Science. We offer undergraduate and graduate degrees in Computer Science and related fields.

About Our Department

The Department of Computer Science at our university offers a wide range of courses, from introductory programming and computer science theory to advanced algorithms and artificial intelligence. Our faculty is comprised of experts in their respective fields, and they are actively engaged in cutting-edge research. The Department of Computer Science also provides resources such as state-of-the-art computer labs, software libraries, and support services for students.

Departmental Research

Our departmental research focuses on areas such as artificial intelligence, machine learning, natural language processing, cybersecurity, and distributed systems. The faculty members publish their research in leading international conferences and journals, and they collaborate with researchers from other institutions and industries. Our students have the opportunity to participate in research projects under the guidance of our faculty members.
